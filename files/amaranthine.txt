This painting is called Amaranthine.
Its artist is Jesper Krijgsman.
Jesper Krijgsman is a self-taught Dutch visual artist.
He carefully assembles his photographed natural elements digitally to create detailed worlds 
that depict scenes in which the mystical qualities of the natural world are magnified.
"For me, the ingenuity of many life forms is something that deserves more attention."
"I consider the creativity in the evolution of flora and fauna to be something magical."
"The harmony in which this diversity coexists gives me a sense of euphoria which I want to show in my work."
